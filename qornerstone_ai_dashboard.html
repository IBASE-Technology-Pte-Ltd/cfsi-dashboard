Scoring Formula
The web app computes a weighted CFSI score from reserve adequacy, arrears pressure, ageing assets, repainting delay, special levy history, and postponed major works. Higher score means higher financial stress.
CFSI Formula
CFSI = 35% Reserve Gap + 20% Arrears + 15% Lift Age + 10% Repainting Delay + 10% Special Levies + 10% Failed Works
Reserve GapBased on how much 10-year capex is not covered by current sinking fund.
ArrearsHigher owner arrears increase liquidity and collection risk.
Lift AgeOlder lifts increase replacement risk and near-term capex pressure.
Repainting DelayLong delays indicate maintenance slippage and future repair cost escalation.
Special LeviesPast levies are treated as evidence of prior reserve stress.
Failed WorksPostponed projects indicate execution strain and deferred liabilities.
Suggested thresholds: below 40 = Stable, 40 to 64 = Watchlist, 65 and above = Stressed. You can later tune the weights to match your internal model.
